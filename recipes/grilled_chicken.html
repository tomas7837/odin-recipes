Grilled Chicken

A healthy and tasty option for a nice meal

This dish is thought with the idea of having something quick for eating


but also a good source of protein to get you going during the day

I'll leave the ingredients here for reference

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 kg of chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Seasoning for flavor (salt, pepper and praprika)
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 half of an onion

Here's the whole steps to prepare this great meal

  1. Cut the chicken in small cubes
  2. Start seasoning with the olive oil and all the good stuff
  3. Start heating the pan on low heat
  4. Once is nice and hot start grilling the now seasoned chicken
  5. WHile the chicken is being cooked, start cutting uo the pepper
  6. After the chicken is starting looking darkish, add the pepper
  7. Then cook for 5 more minutes
  8. Serve with a small salad or a bit of beans
